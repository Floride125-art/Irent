About E-HRent

E-HRent is the company that was etablished in 2018 to facilitate the Tenants to easily get houses for renting. It is an online platform that help people to search and choose the houeses for renting according to their better choices they make.

Why E-HRent?

After carefully consideration we made, we found that many people spend most of their time searching for the perfect houses they can rent which sometimes end up getting nothing because they are looking for something they are not sure about or they get to get what they don't really want. In that case we have decided to come with this company that will help people to easily look for the houses they want with enough details on affordable price. We did not only consider tenants who are looking for houses but also the landlords who need people to rent out their houses whereby it is easy for them to publish their properties for renting so that they can be known everywhere to attract different customers.

Our Mission

To be champions among the companies that offer the same services by making sure that all tenants who are looking houses for rent in Kigali city, can easily find it without so much troubles of problems related to getting houses houses for renting.
